About GA Connects

Our Mission

GA Connects is dedicated to bridging the digital divide in Georgia by connecting communities to high-speed internet and providing the resources needed to thrive in the digital age.

What We Do

Connect Communities

We help Georgians find and access high-speed internet service providers in their area.

Advocate for Change

We work with policymakers and stakeholders to expand internet access across Georgia.

Provide Resources

We offer tools and support to help communities navigate the digital landscape.
